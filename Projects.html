Sea Turtle Projects In Sri Lanka

Kosgoda Sea Turtle Conservation Project

The project's main goal is to monitor local sea turtle activity and protect local nesting places. We want to raise public awareness about how endangered these magnificent species are and how critical it is to help conserve them before it's too late.

Sri Lanka Sea Turtle Conservation

Come and contribute to the most exciting sea turtle initiative in Sri Lanka, the first of its kind! You will play a significant part in the conservation of critically endangered sea turtles on a completely ethical initiative set up and run by The Mighty Roar.

Sea Turtle Conservation Volunteer Program in Sri Lanka

Interested in volunteering in Sri Lanka for sea turtle conservation? Volunteers may contribute to the rehabilitation of wounded turtles by participating in the Sea Turtle Conservation initiative of International Volunteer HQ.
