Donations

Hatchling Hope

Nest Protection: Help us protect sea turtle nests from predators and poachers. Your donation will help us build and maintain sea turtle nesting fences, install motion-sensor lights, and educate the public about the importance of sea turtle conservation.

Journey to the Ocean

Hatchling Release: Help us release baby sea turtles into the ocean. Your donation will help us provide food and water for the hatchlings, and transport them to safe release sites.



Second Life

Turtle Rehabilitation: Help us rehabilitate injured and orphaned sea turtles. Your donation will help us provide medical care, food, and water for the turtles, and help them recover so they can be released back into the wild.


Turtle Guardians

Education: Help us educate the public about sea turtles and the threats they face. Your donation will help us develop and deliver educational programs for schools, community groups, and the general public.
